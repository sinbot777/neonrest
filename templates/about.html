About Neonrest

Neonrest is a people-first social network built for closeness — not chaos. It’s a place to slow down, express something honest, and reconnect with friends in ways that feel human again.

Instead of chasing engagement, we built Neonrest around vibes. You’ll tag posts with emotional tone — not just topics — and settle into neighborhoods that feel like home, like Backporch, Analog, or Driftroom.

We’re blending retro aesthetics and modern tools to create something softer and more intentional. Think beanbags, late-night conversations, mixtapes, and zines — not ads, metrics, or clout.

We don’t track you, we don’t push you to perform, and we’re not here to win the algorithm. This is a network made by people who miss the web when it felt like ours.
